# CR3DT 
`Camera-RADAR 3D Detection and Tracking (CR3DT)` is a camera-radar fusion model for 3D object detection and multi-object tracking.

![CR3DT|400](https://notes-media.kthiha.com/CR3DT-(2024)/a2265c3229180d68729ffdb25eae2018.png)

It builds upon `BEVDet` architecture by incorporating spatial & velocity information of the [[Radar|Radar sensor]].
