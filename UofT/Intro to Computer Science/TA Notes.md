`Encouraging`
- Treat each question as good question
- Guide the students to the right answer
- Help them problem solve.
  Can u tell me what steps u have tried?
- Interesting Delivery
- Asking Questions
- Give fun examples

`Discouraging`
- Don't directly give answer
- Make a reasonable assumption xD
- Focusing on grades + POST
- Skipping over content "already known/easy"
- Don't let them be unengaged


`Notes`
- If students don't answer question, just wait ...
  Silence is golden.
- Make obviously wrong answer xD
- If student is not focused (like playing games), let them know u dun like it, but dun make it feel like being singled out


`Bad Things`
- Talking to the board
- Not repeating
- Bad handwriting
- Not asking questions
- No motivation
- No narration
