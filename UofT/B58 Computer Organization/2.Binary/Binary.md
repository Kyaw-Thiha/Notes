## Conversions
### Binary to Hexadecimal
From right to left,
- group them in groups of $4$.
- convert each one to hex.

### Decimal to Binary
- Repeatedly divide the number by $2$.
- Append the remainder to the left of your binary number.
- Repeat until your quotient ends up being $0$.

![|300](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/QAHyFa3gKKc/hq720.jpg?sqp=-oaymwEhCK4FEIIDSFryq4qpAxMIARUAAAAAGAElAADIQj0AgKJD&rs=AOn4CLAtRwTOO0WP-S6J2ia8BVZqf64UuA)

---
### Binary to Decimal
- Place $2^{n}$ for each $n^{th}$ position of the binary.
- If $1$, add the equivalent $2^{n}$.

![|300](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/1*R51d6XO7Sd3r72WZam5ewA.png)

---
### Decimal to Hexadecimal
- Repeatedly divide the number by $16$.
- Append the remainder to the left of the hexadecimal number.
- Repeat until your quotient becomes $0$.

![|300](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/QJW6qnfhC70/maxresdefault.jpg)

---
### Hexadecimal to Decimal
- Place $16^{n}$ for each $n^{th}$ position of the binary.
- If $1$, add the equivalent $16^{n}$.
![|300](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/pg-HEGBpCQk/maxresdefault.jpg)
