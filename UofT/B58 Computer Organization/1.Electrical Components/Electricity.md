# Electricity
[[Electricity]] is the flow of electrons through a material.

- Protons have a $(+)$ charge.
- Electrons have a $(-)$ charge.
- In a regular atom, proton count $=$ electron count

---
## Flow of Electrons
Electrons want to flow from regions of high electrical potential to regions of low electrical potential.

This electrical potential is called **voltage**.

Potential difference pushes a current, which goes down the higher the resistance.
$$
V = IR
$$
The current flowing depends on how smooth the path is.

---
### Battery running out of power
In battery, the voltage is the difference in potential between the negative and positive side.

The potential difference does not go down the more you use it.
Instead, it suddenly drops when the battery runs out.
