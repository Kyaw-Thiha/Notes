# Capacitor
A [[capacitor]] is a passive electronic component that 
- temporarily stores electrical energy in an electric field 
- by accumulating charge on two conductive plates 
- separated by an insulating material called dielectric.

![Capacitor|300](https://theengineeringmindset.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/capacitor-basics.png)

> Unlike batteries, capacitors store energy in an electric field and can release it very quickly.
