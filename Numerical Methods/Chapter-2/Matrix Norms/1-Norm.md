# 1-Norm
`Definition`
To find a `1-Norm`, we choose the maximum of absolute column sum.

`Example`

Let $A = \begin{bmatrix}.780 & .563 \\ .913 & .659\end{bmatrix}$

- Column-1: $|0.780| + |0.913| = 1.693$
- Column-2: $|0.563| + |0.659| = 1.222$

Taking the maximum value,
$$
||A||_{1} = max(1.693, 1.222) = 1.693
$$

`Answer`: $||A||_{1} = 1.693$
