# Infinity Norm
`Definition`
To find an `Infinity Norm`, we choose the maximum of absolute row sum.

`Example`

Let $A = \begin{bmatrix}.780 & .563 \\ .913 & .659\end{bmatrix}$

- Row-1: $|0.780| + |0.563| = 1.343$
- Row-2: $|0.913| + |0.659| = 1.572$

Taking the maximum value,
$$
||A||_{\infty} = max(1.343, 1.572) = 1.572
$$

`Answer`: $||A||_{\infty} = 1.572$
