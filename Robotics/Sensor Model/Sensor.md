# Sensor
#robotics/sensor 
A `sensor` is a device which measures a specific physical quantity.

![Sensor|400](https://robocraze.com/cdn/shop/articles/What_Is_a_Sensor_Types_Uses_Working_Explained_f9045299-1f6f-4dc2-a85c-9388b8775a2c.webp?v=1744367644)


`Properties of Good Sensors`
- Sensitive to physical quantity being measured
- Insensitive to other physical quantity encountered during use.
- Doesn't change value of physical quantity being measured by significant amount.

---
## Sensor Types
The `sensor` can be electronic, mechanical, chemical or something else.

![Different Sensors](https://www.hackatronic.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Types-of-Sensors-.webp)

There are many types of sensors to collect different data.
