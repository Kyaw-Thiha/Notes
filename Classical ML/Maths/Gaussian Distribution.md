## PDF
$$
f_X(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi \alpha^2}} 
\exp\!\left( -\frac{(x - \mu)^2}{2\alpha^2} \right),
\quad x \in \mathbb{R},
$$

where
- $\mu = \mathbb{E}[X] \in \mathbb{R}, \quad$
- $\alpha^2 = \mathrm{Var}(X) = \mathbb{E}[(X - \mu)^2] \in \mathbb{R}.$
